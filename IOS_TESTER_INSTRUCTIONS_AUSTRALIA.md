## üì± Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Install TestFlight App

1. Open the **App Store** on your iPhone 11
2. Search for **"TestFlight"**
3. Install the **TestFlight** app (it's free, made by Apple)
4. Open TestFlight after installation

---

### Step 2: Wait for Email Invitation

You'll receive an email invitation from Apple/TestFlight that looks like:

**Subject:** "You're invited to test TrueScan"

**From:** noreply@email.apple.com or App Store Connect

**The email will contain:**
- A link to open TestFlight
- Instructions to install the app

**Note:** This email will be sent after the developer adds you as a tester (see developer instructions below).

---

### Step 3: Accept Invitation in TestFlight

1. **Option A - From Email:**
   - Tap the link in the invitation email
   - It will open TestFlight automatically
   - Tap "Accept" or "View in TestFlight"

2. **Option B - From TestFlight App:**
   - Open the TestFlight app
   - You'll see "TrueScan" in the "Available to Test" section
   - Tap on it

---

### Step 4: Install the App

1. In TestFlight, you'll see the TrueScan app
2. Tap **"Install"** or **"Update"** button
3. The app will install on your iPhone
4. You'll see the TrueScan app icon on your home screen

---

6. Go back and open the TrueScan app

## ‚ùì Troubleshooting

### "App won't install"
- Make sure you have enough storage space
- Check your internet connection
- Try restarting your iPhone

### "Untrusted Developer" error
- Follow Step 5 above to trust the developer
- This only needs to be done once

### "TestFlight invitation expired"
- Contact the developer to resend invitation
- Invitations expire after 90 days

### "Can't find TestFlight app"
- Search "TestFlight" in App Store
- It's a free app by Apple
